How It Works

SSPred submits your sequence to the selected remote predictors in parallel, stores each response as it arrives, and recomputes the majority-vote line during the run.

Typical Wait Times (150 aa sequence)

  • PSI: 1-7 minutes
  • JPred: 3-5 minutes
  • Sable: 5-7 minutes
  • SSPro: ~8 minutes
  • NetSurf: often 20-25 minutes

Prediction Services

  • PSI: PSIPRED
  • JPred: JPred4
  • Sable: SABLE
  • SSPro: SSPro
  • NetSurf: NetSurfP-2.0
  • RCSB: Protein Data Bank
