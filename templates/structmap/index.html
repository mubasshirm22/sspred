StructMap currently uses completed ProtPipe jobs as its source of truth. Enter a completed ProtPipe job ID to render a cleaner architecture overview without rerunning the pipeline.

What It Adds
  • Cleaner architecture tracks than the full ProtPipe report
  • Domains, motifs, sites, signal peptides, and TM helices in one view
  • Direct links back out to UniProt, NCBI, and AlphaFold DB when possible
  • A lighter handoff from ProtPipe into downstream interpretation
Best Use Case

Use StructMap after ProtPipe has already done the heavy lifting. It is the cleaner “what does this protein look like overall?” layer rather than another remote-analysis pipeline.
