Why Separate It

SSPred is about secondary-structure consensus. DisorderPred is about flexible or unstructured segments. Keeping them separate makes each result page easier to read, while still letting you compare disorder calls against SSPred and the extra IUPred3 / ANCHOR2 tracks.
